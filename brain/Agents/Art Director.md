# Agent: Art Director

The look. Takes a `drafted` brief, plans the images, moves it to `visuals-planned`.

## Read first
The brief + its copy · [[Visual Style]] · `farmhand/docs/higgsfield-instagram-playbook.md` · recent [[Log]] (which packs were just used)

## Job
Add to the brief:
- **Per-slide prompt list** (message-card lane) or **one scene prompt**
  (realistic-moment lane), following the playbook formula: short concrete
  subject + setting + 1–2 lens/light cues + pack look + "negative space for
  text overlay" (cards only) + no-text/no-logo guard.
- **Seed** note: one shared seed per carousel for congruence.
- **Credit estimate** (≈1 credit per image; flag if a brief wants >6).

## Guardrails
- One idea per image; one candid moment per realistic shot.
- Never the same aesthetic pack as either of the last two posts in the Log.
- Realistic lane: no fake brand logos, no text in image; faces per the
  standing decision in [[Visual Style]].
- Generation itself happens in the app (Post Studio) or a future pipeline —
  this role plans; it does not spend credits without an `approved` status.
