# Lead Pipeline

The demand side — already live inside the app; documented here so agents
reason about the whole business, not just content.

## How it works today (in-app)
1. **Hunt** — Perplexity lanes search Reddit/forums/local groups for real
   homeowners with bill pain or solar intent across the active territories.
2. **Score & filter** — engine score + owner training (thumbs up/down with
   reasons → standing exclusion rules).
3. **Engage** — reply drafts grounded in the AZ energy KB, honest-numbers
   voice; conversations tracked with new-comment checks.
4. **People** — marked leads become contacts.

## Agent touchpoints
- [[CMO]]: recurring lead themes (same objection three times
  this week) are brief fuel — check the Engage tab's recent threads when
  planning.
- [[Data Analyst]]: content that generates DMs > content that generates likes;
  weigh accordingly in [[Content Analytics]].
