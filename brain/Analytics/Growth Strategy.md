## Audit findings

1. **The bio sells; the strategy educates.** "Helping homeowners go solar
   + DM for a consultation" is a salesperson bio. The whole brand thesis
   is resource-first. Proposed rewrite (owner approves before changing):
   - `Arizona power bills & solar, explained honestly`
   - `APS & SRP rate breakdowns · quote red flags · no pitch`
   - `📲 DM "AZ" for a free rate-plan check`
   Keep the "DM AZ" keyword funnel — it's genuinely good — but attach it
   to a free VALUE action (rate-plan check), not "a consultation".
2. **"Trusted by 100+ Residents" on a 1-post page** reads as unverifiable
   puffery to a skeptical homeowner — and skepticism is our audience's
   defining trait (distrust doubled to 44%). The honest-numbers brand
   earns more than the claim does. Recommend dropping or replacing with
   something checkable.
3. **"Virtual Solar"** in the display name doesn't communicate the
   advisor position and wastes searchable words. IG name field is
   searchable: "Taylor Luce | AZ Solar & Energy Advisor" surfaces for
   "AZ solar" searches.
4. **261 following vs 133 followers** reads growth-hacky at a glance.
   Slowly trim following toward ~parity; follow local accounts with
   purpose (realtors, city pages, AZ journalists) not reciprocity.
5. **Format gap vs the reach goal**: all 5 queued posts are static/
   carousel. Reels are IG's non-follower discovery engine — the "outward
   facing reach" lever — and the account's single existing post is
   (correctly) a reel. The queue needs a reel lane.

## The reach model (three engines, weekly rhythm)

1. **Reels = reach** (2–3/week): 15–30s, one idea, big summarized text,
   the education in the caption. First candidates: reel cuts of the five
   queued topics (the 40¢ window, the 3 questions, the October cliff).
   Until DoP video generation is wired, reels = Taylor talking or simple
   motion over stills exported from the Studio.
2. **Carousels = saves** (1–2/week): the Studio's card posts. Saves and
   shares are the signals IG rewards — every carousel ends on a
   save-worthy payoff (already our model).
3. **Presence = borrowed audiences** (daily 15 min): useful, non-pitchy
   comments on big AZ accounts (news, city pages, realtor educators) —
   same philosophy as the app's lead engine, different venue. Plus
   **Collab posts** with local realtors/lenders/inspectors — a collab
   publishes to BOTH audiences, the single fastest follower borrow a
   local account can do. The realtor-audit list doubles as the collab
   prospect list.

Support layer: geotag every post (rotate Valley cities) · local hashtag
stack (already standard on queue posts) · Stories on posting days with a
poll/question sticker · Highlights once ~10 posts exist ("Start here",
"APS vs SRP", "Quote check") · saved reply wired to the "AZ" DM keyword.

## Measurement (Analyst, weekly once posting starts)

Track in [[Content Analytics]]: follower count · reach + non-follower
reach % per post · saves/shares per post · DMs with keyword "AZ" ·
profile visits → follows conversion. Adjust format mix toward whatever
the non-follower reach data says.
